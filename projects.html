DEEJ

Used technologies: Arduino, DEEJ-Software

For my DEEJ Audiomixer with Arduino build, I embarked on an exciting project that combines hardware and software to create a dynamic audio mixing device. Utilizing an Arduino microcontroller, I designed and programmed the system to handle multiple audio inputs, allowing for seamless mixing and control through physical knobs and sliders. The project involved intricate wiring and careful calibration to ensure high-quality sound output. This hands-on experience not only enhanced my understanding of electronic components and circuitry but also showcased my ability to integrate hardware with software to produce a functional and innovative audio mixing solution.
